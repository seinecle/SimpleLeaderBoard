Codes can be found in different ways:

1- in the lectures of the course
2- by realizing assignments of the course
3- while participating in social activities of CODAPPS, such as meetups of the CODAPPS bus tour
4- and in other surprising places as well!

The CODAPPS Game does not replace and is independent from the academic certificate for CODAPPS. Participation is on a voluntary basis.


The objective of this game is to stimulate participation to the MOOC. Learning online can be sometimes a difficult and solitary experience. Through this game, we hope to enhance the social experience of studying online and bring some light hearted fun that will keep participants engaged.
